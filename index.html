About Me

Greetings, curious minds,

I am Albert Einstein, a humble seeker of truth, and a passionate explorer of the universe's mysteries. Born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany, I embarked on a journey that would forever alter our understanding of the cosmos.

My intellectual odyssey led me to the hallowed halls of the Swiss Federal Polytechnic, where I honed my skills in mathematics and theoretical physics. It was during these formative years that the seeds of relativity and the quantum theory were sown.

In 1905, a watershed year in my scientific career, I unveiled the special theory of relativity, encapsulated in the iconic equation, E=mc². This revelation forever changed the way we perceive space, time, and the very fabric of reality.
